Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Overview

Astra Relay is a rhythm puzzle game. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the game and how to contact the developer.

Information We Process

The game may store local gameplay progress, settings, scores, level completion, and tool counts on your device. The game may also process device identifiers and advertising-related signals when advertising or analytics SDKs are enabled after required consent and configuration.

Advertising and Analytics

The app may use third-party advertising and analytics services to show ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and improve the game experience. Advertising services are initialized only when valid runtime configuration is available and, where required, after the App Tracking Transparency prompt is completed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited technical data may be shared with service providers that support advertising, measurement, security, and app operation.

Children

The game is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it when appropriate.

Your Choices

You can adjust device privacy settings, advertising tracking permissions, notification permissions, and game audio settings through iOS or in-game controls where available.
